SOUND WAVES CARRY

ENERGY THROUGH AIR TO

OUR EARS

In today's world, highly and influenced by technology, one can wonder if music (being such a primitive form of expression) can still be worthy of making?

MY ANSWER

IS YES

Mankind, although using different forms and mediums, will always need music whether for entertaining or spiritual purposes (or both).

BASED IN LONDON

I enjoy working in different fields ranging from abstract/electronic music to visual media but my goal is always the same: To let that energy affect you and allow you to experience something different than what our daily lives offer us. Stay tuned and feel free to get in touch.
